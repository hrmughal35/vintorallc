Admin Panel Features

Dashboard

The main page when you log into admin. It shows:

Categories Management

Organize your products into categories. For example: Paper Cups, Containers, Cutlery, etc.

Viewing Categories:

  1. Click "Categories" in the left menu
  2. You will see all your product categories
  3. Each category can have subcategories

Adding a New Category:

  1. Click "Categories" in the menu
  2. Click "Add New Category" button
  3. Enter the category name (for example: "Paper Cups")
  4. Add a description if you want
  5. Click "Save"

Editing a Category:

  1. Go to Categories page
  2. Find the category you want to edit
  3. Click the "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes
  5. Click "Save"

Adding Subcategories:

  1. Go to Categories page
  2. Find the category where you want to add a subcategory
  3. Click "Add Subcategory"
  4. Enter the subcategory name
  5. Click "Save"

Products Management

Add, edit, and organize all your products.

Viewing Products:

  1. Click "Products" in the left menu
  2. You will see all your products organized by category
  3. Products are grouped under their categories and subcategories

Adding a New Product:

  1. Click "Products" in the menu
  2. Click "Add New Product" button
  3. Select the category and subcategory
  4. Enter product name
  5. Add product description
  6. Add product specifications (size, material, etc.)
  7. Add product images if available
  8. Click "Save"

Editing a Product:

  1. Go to Products page
  2. Find the product you want to edit
  3. Click "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes
  5. Click "Save"

Deleting a Product:

  1. Go to Products page
  2. Find the product you want to delete
  3. Click "Delete" button
  4. Confirm the deletion

Blog Management

Create and manage blog posts to share news and information.

Viewing Blog Posts:

  1. Click "Blog" in the left menu
  2. You will see a list of all your blog posts
  3. Each post shows the title, date, and status

Creating a New Blog Post:

  1. Click "Blog" in the menu
  2. Click "Create New Post" button
  3. Enter the blog post title
  4. Write your blog content
  5. Add images if you want
  6. Set if this post should be featured on the homepage
  7. Click "Save" or "Publish"

Editing a Blog Post:

  1. Go to Blog page
  2. Find the post you want to edit
  3. Click "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes
  5. Click "Save"

Deleting a Blog Post:

  1. Go to Blog page
  2. Find the post you want to delete
  3. Click "Delete" button
  4. Confirm the deletion

Contact Messages

View and manage messages sent by customers through your contact form.

Viewing Messages:

  1. Click "Contacts" in the left menu
  2. You will see a list of all messages from customers
  3. New messages will have a red badge showing the number
  4. Click on any message to read it

Reading a Message:

  1. Go to Contacts page
  2. Click on any message in the list
  3. The full message will open on the right side
  4. You will see: customer name, email, phone, subject, and message

Marking Messages as Read:

  1. When you read a message, it is automatically marked as read
  2. You can also manually mark messages as read or unread
  3. This helps you keep track of which messages you have responded to

Deleting Messages:

  1. Go to Contacts page
  2. Find the message you want to delete
  3. Click "Delete" button
  4. Confirm the deletion

Admin Panel Navigation

  • Dashboard: Overview of your website statistics

  • Products: Manage all your products
  • Blog: Manage blog posts
  • Contacts: View customer messages
  • View Site: Opens your public website in a new tab
  • Logout: Sign out of admin panel

Important Tips

Always Save Your Changes

After making any changes, always click the "Save" button. If you navigate away without saving, your changes will be lost.

Check Your Website After Changes

After adding or editing products or blog posts, click "View Site" to see how it looks to your customers.

Respond to Messages

Check your Contacts section regularly to respond to customer inquiries. Mark messages as read after you respond.

Keep Your Content Updated

Regularly update your products, add new blog posts, and keep your information current. This helps with search engines and keeps customers engaged.
